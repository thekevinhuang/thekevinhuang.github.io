Something that I've been meaning to do is explore some of the principles of software engineering in the scope of the real world. At its core, writing code is a way to solve problems. From doing some simple calculations, to managing the purchases of millions of people, software engineering is just creating solutions to tackle challenges. People have been solving problems for long before computers even came into the picture.
