My Background

During my 8 years of management consulting leading projects and creating business solutions, I gained experiences tackling and solving problems in many industries. Most recently, I have been working extensively in the Healthcare field working with The Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS), helping facilitate insurance payments impacting over 11 million Americans.

It was during this time that I rekindled my interest in software engineering. What had initially started in high school with creating programs for graphing calculators, later grew into writing applications in Java. After laying dormant for a period, coding didn't resurface in my skillset until I started created solutions for my clients. I ended up creating macros in VBA and dabbling in Python. The impact was more consistent, faster, and more reliable analyses. I once again was drawn to the power of code and software as a solution.

Now fully recognizing the importance of technology and engineering in all industries, I decided to commit to expanding this skillset and enrolled in The Flatiron School's online web development program. I learned useful and modern tools for web development such as React with Redux, JS, Ruby on Rails, HTML, and CSS. I am hoping to apply these technologies to creating more powerful solutions to more complicated problems.
