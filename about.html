About Me

I am a born problem solver and joker who enjoys nature and creating in many different forms - from photography to cooking, and of course coding.

I am a person who enjoys nature and being active. I enjoy cycling, for a few hours or a couple days in a row. If riding is inconvenient, or for shorter distances, running is good as well. For steeper surfaces, a nice slow hike is preferred. If any of these activities are topped off with a refreshing beer or a good meal, they are all the more enjoyable. I also like to document my travels and activities and am usually accompanied by my DSLR (and more recently my smartphone).

When I am indoors, I enjoy experimenting with new ingredients in the kitchen, tinkering on DIY projects, and tending to my indoor forest. I try to keep up with current films and developments in the video game industry. I also quite enjoy a bad joke or two.
